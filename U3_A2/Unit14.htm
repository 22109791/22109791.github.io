About Unit 14

This is the unit 14 page (Installing and Maintaining Computer Hardware). Unit 14 is about developing an understanding of the benefits and implications of installing and maintaining hardware in a technology system. This involved installing a PCie 3.0 Express Card as well as new RAM sticks. I also replaced the CMos battery. At the time of creating this e-portfolio I have completed 2/3 assignments for this unit.
