Unit 15

About Unit 15

This is the unit 15 page (Installing and Maintaining Computer Software). Unit 15 is about developing an understanding of the benefits and implications of installing and maintaining software in a technology system. This involved installing and upgrading an operating system and different software applications, such as office or multimedia programs. I also had to customise different components of software applications to improve productivity, for example to create new toolbars and automate a range of tasks using a macro.The technology system then needed to be tested for functionality,usability and productivity. At the time of creating this e-portfolio I have completed 2/3 assignments for this unit.
